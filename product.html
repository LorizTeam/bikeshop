Yamaha R6

The Yamaha YZF-R6 is without a doubt one of the most wanted motorcycles in its class. And there is no wonder why, as it is fitted with a wide range of modern technologies which help it deliver superior ride and handling performances. At its heart sits a liquid-cooled inline 4-cylinder; DOHC, 16 Titanium Valves engine with a displacement of 599 cc. Among the technologies offered by the motorcycles you will find YCC-T, YCC-I, a slipper type clutch and a close ration 6 speed transmission. The motorcycle is built on a Deltabox aluminum frame which is combined with a magnesium subframe and a lightweight swingarm. The wheels are kept in check by a four-way adjustable (preload, high-speed compression, low-speed compression and rebound damping) inverted fork with 41mm tubes and a four-way adjustable (preload, high-speed compression, low-speed compression and rebound damping) piggyback rear shock.
